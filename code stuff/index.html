Resident Evil 2 is the best horror game

Explanation 1

Resident Evil 2 (RE2) is considered one of the best horror video games due to its perfect blend of survival horror and compelling storytelling. The game's eerie atmosphere, tight controls, and world. RE2 masterfully builds tension with limited resources, making every encounter nerve-wracking. The dual narrative, where players can choose to play as Leon or Claire, offers varied perspectives and replayability. With improved graphics, intelligent AI, and a chilling soundtrack, Resident Evil 2 captures the essence of horror while offering a gripping, unforgettable experience for fans of the genre.
